Neural Compression Correction

Add images either by URL or by loading from local file. Once an image is loaded, choose the action under the image. Once the action is complete, the new image will be loaded. You can then continue to apply actions to that image.

Actions

Correct Compression
Attempts to undue the damage done by lossy compression.
"Enhance"
Doubles the resolution of the image and then applies the "Correct Compression" action.
